Mind Matters

Mental health isn’t just about feeling sad or stressed — it’s about how we think, feel, and handle life every day. For teens especially, taking care of mental health is crucial.

According to the CDC, more than 1 in 3 high school students reported experiencing persistent feelings of sadness or hopelessness in 2021. Nearly 1 in 5 seriously considered suicide. These numbers aren’t just statistics — they’re a reminder that you’re not alone, and that your mental health matters just as much as your physical health.

Understanding mental health is the first step toward breaking stigma, building coping skills, and finding support. This space is here to help you do just that.
